Tools

  • DLR_ICF: https://github.com/BenxiaHu/DLR_ICF - Analyzes distal-to-local ratio (DLR) and inter-chromosomal fraction (ICF).
  • MutSeq: https://github.com/BenxiaHu/MutSeq - Generates in silico genetic/point mutations based on given DNA sequences.
  • ExpreLev: https://github.com/BenxiaHu/ExpreLev - Obtains TPM/CPM/FPKM for genes or peak regions based on raw read count matrices.
  • FullMatrix: https://github.com/BenxiaHu/FullMatrix - Generates a full matrix for each chromosome.
